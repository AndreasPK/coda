data Dyn a Source #

A functional version of Overmars and Van Leeuwen's 1983 dynamization schema.

If your queries are monoid homomorphisms from a this takes a static structure with an O(n) (<>) and gives you dynamic one, with O(log n) slower queries, but O(log n) (<>).
